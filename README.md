# 🛒 E-commerce Analytics & Customer Insights

### SQL-Based Data Analysis using PostgreSQL | Olist Dataset

---

## 📌 Executive Summary

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of an e-commerce dataset to derive actionable insights into revenue performance, customer behavior, delivery efficiency, and seller contribution.

Using PostgreSQL, over 100K+ orders and 300K+ transaction records were analyzed to support data-driven decision-making and identify key business opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Evaluate overall sales performance and revenue growth trends
* Identify high-value customers and analyze purchasing behavior
* Assess delivery efficiency and its impact on customer satisfaction
* Analyze product and seller performance to optimize operations

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Overview

* **Source:** Olist E-commerce Dataset (Brazil)
* **Data Volume:** 100,000+ orders | 300,000+ records
* **Data Model Includes:**

  * Customers & Orders
  * Payments & Order Items
  * Products & Categories
  * Sellers & Geolocation
  * Customer Reviews

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **Database:** PostgreSQL
* **Query Language:** SQL
* **Visualization:** Power BI (for dashboarding)
* **Data Preparation:** Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Analytical Approach

### 🔹 Sales & Revenue Analysis

* Monthly revenue trend and growth rate (MoM analysis)
* Average Order Value (AOV) and revenue distribution
* Identification of top-performing product categories

### 🔹 Customer Intelligence

* Customer segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
* Churn and retention analysis
* Identification of high-value and repeat customers

### 🔹 Product & Seller Performance

* Top-selling products and revenue-generating categories
* Seller contribution analysis using percentage share
* Performance benchmarking across sellers

### 🔹 Delivery & Operations Analysis

* Average delivery time and delay analysis
* Late delivery rate and operational bottlenecks
* Impact of delivery delays on customer satisfaction

### 🔹 Customer Experience Analysis

* Review score distribution and trend analysis
* Correlation between delivery performance and ratings

---

## 🔥 Key Business Insights

* A small segment (~10%) of customers contributes a major share of total revenue
* Late deliveries are strongly correlated with lower customer ratings
* High-performing product categories drive the majority of sales
* Repeat customers significantly enhance long-term revenue stability
